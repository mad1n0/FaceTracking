What personal data do we collect about you and how?

The App's functionalities require the collection of personal data. Sometimes you provide us with data, sometimes data about you is collected or inferred through your use of the App or generated by us through analysis.

Since our service is focused on the development of a framework for visual attention tracking for portable devices, some of the personal information that you share, or we collect from you, might be related to Biometric Data. See our Terms & Conditions.

When you create an account within the App, you share with us the following information:
1) Name
2) Email Address.

When you use the App and complete the designated tasks by using the phone's camera, you share with us the following information:
1) Head position and orientation: we will extract a matrix of numbers in order to generate vectors that evaluate your interaction with the mobile phone;
2) Eyes position and orientation: we will extract a matrix of numbers in order to generate vector that evaluate your interaction with the mobile phone;
3) Eye blinking: we will generate a number between 0 and 1 that indicates how closely are you to have your eyes fully shut or open;
4) Eyebrows position: we will generate a number between 0 and 1 that indicates how closely are you to have your eyebrows fully raised or relaxed;
5) Jaw position: we will extract a number between 0 and 1 that indicates how closely are you to have your jaw fully open or closed;
6) Shape of the environment: we use a module (ARKit), that uses the information provided by the rear camera to generate a 3D model of the surrounding environment. This model can be used to place imaginary models for video games and such or to have a more accurate idea of how the device is moving in order to track better your position;
7) Acceleration of the device: we will capture the device acceleration of the device by accessing the accelerometer of the device using SensinfKit, a framework which captures sensor data form iOS devices. The captured data has the shape of a series of decimal numbers that represent accelerations applied to the device.

When you use the App and answer our questionnaires and tests, you share with us the following information:
1) Information related to the App usage, such as interaction data;
2) Demographics information, such as your age group and your gender;
3) Your opinion on the App and its functionality including, but not limited to, technical issues.

UNDERSTANDING THE APP

The App is intended to develop a framework for visual attention tracking for portable devices like smartphones and tablets. This tool is of particular value to mobile phone users, since it will allow for a new type of interactions with portable devices, without the need for touch gestures, hence enabling - for example - people with disabilities to successfully complete tasks on their mobile phone device using their gaze.

By using this App, you will interact with a mobile phone in order to complete a set of simple tasks that involve looking at the mobile phone screen and following with your gaze a marker or symbol. In addition, during the study, we may ask you to fill out a number of questionnaires and collect demographic information or other feedback.

Keep in mind that we will not be evaluating your performance in any way. Therefore, we encourage you to do this at your own pace and enjoy the process. Finally, it is advised that, while you engage with the tasks, to try and not to let yourself get distracted.
